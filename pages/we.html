      History

      We started with the dream of building a community around art in our neighborhood with the intention of teaching kids a way of life apart from violence and family troubles.
      We cleaned up the garage and made some pieces to can invite our neighbors to our first presentation. Since, more people have visited us and have suscribed to our workshops, so we decided get a bigger place to teach, and make cool expositions.

      We

      Curtis Cole

      I was art teacher in a rural school for five years. When I came back to the city I wanted to start a project with social impact and I thought that the best place to start was right there in my house with my wife.

      Woodrow Padberg

      I was literature teacher and a great sculpture enthusiast, the moment that Curtis told me about this new project I left my work to be part of the sculpture school that there's in Valdor Gallery.
